Game Jams

Home Office Simulator

Jun 2021 - miniBeansjam 7

After more than a year of the pandemic, most people are familiar with the home office situation. Twice as much work to do at home, but only half as little time.

You slept longer than you thought, and oh man, your partner is coming home in two minutes! Quickly read the note on the bedside table so you know what to do and then get to work!

Hyperspace Technician

Aug 2021 - Opera GX Game Jam

You are a space technician who has to get the interdimensional internet up and running again.

To do this, you have to connect the satellites in the asteroid belt. But watch out for the asteroids! If they hit you, your spaceship will be damaged and you will have to start the connection all over again.

Luckily, the laser cannon is charged, so you'd better use it. Supposedly there are also power-ups in the asteroid belt that can help you.

Three Things Three Places All At Once

Mai 2022 - Low Effort Jam 22

A homage to what is probably the best film of 2022.

It doesn't look like much, but that's intentional. The aim of this jam was to design a game with as little effort as possible.

Switch back and forth between three dimensions to find your way through the labyrinth. Collect all three keys to open the locked gates. And don't forget where the portals lead you on your way!

My Master's Degree Project

Introduction to Game Audio

2022 - HTWK Leipzig

The topic of my master thesis was "The development of a prototype in Unity as a guide for the implementation of audio in games".


This resulted in a written guide in which I dealt with the topics of HRTF, occlusion, reverb, ambisonics, distance effects and dynamic music, among others. In addition, I developed a Unity project that I used to demonstrate the practical implementation of these topics.

Other Projects

The Murders in the Rue Morgue

2021/22 - HTWK Leipzig

Cross-media project that ran over two semesters and was realized in collaboration with five other students.

The results include a website, a podcast, a video series, an Instagram account as well as a print template and an enhanced eBook version of the original work.

I created the website and the podcast in particular.

Record, key, track

2021 - HTWK Leipzig

Examination project for the course Compositing. This short clip was shot in the TV studio of the HTWK Leipzig with a camera crane. Post-processing, especially keying, rotoscoping and camera tracking, was done with Fusion by Blackmagic Design.

The scene background was created with Blender.
