Other Projects

Record, key, track

2021 - HTWK Leipzig

Examination project for the course Compositing. This short clip was shot in the TV studio of the HTWK Leipzig with a camera crane. Post-processing, especially keying, rotoscoping and camera tracking, was done with Fusion by Blackmagic Design.

The scene background was created with Blender.

The Murders in the Rue Morgue

2021/22 - HTWK Leipzig

Cross-media project that ran over two semesters and was realized in collaboration with five other students.

The results include a website, a podcast, a video series, an Instagram account as well as a print template and an enhanced eBook version of the original work.

I created the website and the podcast in particular.

How does the brain react to beauty?

2021 - HTWK Leipzig

Examination project for the course Computer Animation in Blender. The animation was created in collaboration with four other students for an exhibition project of the Museology students.

A Handful Of Bullets

2020 - HTWK Leipzig

In this project, a handful of office scenes shot by other students had to be reworked into a western-like film.

The main focus was on the appropriate sound design, which had to be completely redone by us.

Because we had so much fun with this project, we really tried to set every detail in the scene to music and also shot an extra scene ourselves to round off the story.

Of course, a Mexican Standoff is a must in a real Western!

Radio play

2020 - HTWK Leipzig

This radio play was created for the course Sound Design and tells the story of the little dragon "Kokosnuss" as he travels around the world.

The text is based on the story "Der kleine Drache Kokosnuss reist um die Welt" by Ingo Siegner.
